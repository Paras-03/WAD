Top Tourist Places in India

Taj Mahal, Agra

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, built by Shah Jahan in memory of Mumtaz Mahal.

Hampi, Karnataka

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its ancient temples and ruins of Vijayanagar Empire.

Alleppey Backwaters, Kerala

Famous for its scenic houseboat rides through the lush backwaters of Kerala.

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The holiest Gurdwara and the most important pilgrimage site of Sikhism.

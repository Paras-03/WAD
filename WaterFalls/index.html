Explore India's Stunning Waterfalls

From the misty Western Ghats to the lush Northeast

Dudhsagar Falls, Goa

Take a train to Kulem station and trek or hire a jeep to the falls through Bhagwan Mahaveer Sanctuary.

Jog Falls, Karnataka

Reach via Sagara or Shimoga, then drive or take a bus to the viewpoint of Jog Falls.

Nohkalikai Falls, Meghalaya

From Shillong, drive 54 km to Cherrapunji and follow signs to the viewpoint.

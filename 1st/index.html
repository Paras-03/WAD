Welcome to Pune

Explore the historical and modern landmarks of Pune

Shaniwar Wada

A majestic 18th-century fortification and palace built by the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire.

Aga Khan Palace

An iconic palace built in 1892 that served as a prison for Mahatma Gandhi during the freedom struggle.

Sinhagad Fort

A historic hilltop fortress known for its strategic importance and the legendary battle of Tanaji Malusare.
